software engineering is boring 80% of the time
and nobody talks about it

you debug the same thing for hours, sometimes days
stuck at a screen for 12-16 hours straight

you don't always get to build AI-native vertical B2B SaaS
sometimes it's a CRUD app
and you just have to make it really good

sometimes it's ux
sometimes it's a boring feature nobody will notice
but... you just have to do it

i've over-engineered plenty of solutions in my head
redis for caching, vector db for embeddings, graph db for relationships
just to realize postgres can handle all of it
better AND cheaper

a small restaurant in vancouver doesn't need 10 agents analyzing how they place their chairs
they need to know which dish sells best and which day they perform worst
so they can market around that

the data pipeline is the hard part
the llm is the easy one
and the LEAST significant part of the process

you'll spend more time cleaning data and organizing schema
than orchestrating agents

and honestly? that's... fine

i got into this to help people make their lives easier with software
that means building what THEY need
not what i think is cool and then convincing them they need it

no matter how sexy the solution looks to me
if it doesn't solve their problem
it's a side project, not a client project

software doesn't need to be sexy
it just needs to WORK
