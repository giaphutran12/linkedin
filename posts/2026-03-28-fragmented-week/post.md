some weeks mess with your head

you ship all week
and still feel behind

that's been me lately

i've been bouncing between 3 different projects
waiting on agents
fixing bugs
cleaning up edge cases
pushing one thing forward
then switching to the next before the win even lands

from the outside
it probably looks productive

from the inside
it feels like unfinished business everywhere

i think that's one of the weirdest parts of startup engineering

not every week gives you a clean demo
or one shiny launch post

sometimes the work is real
but the proof is fragmented

a bug fix here
an integration there
a pipeline unblocked
a feature still half done in the next repo

you did more than you FEEL

the feeling just hasn't caught up yet

still learning how to deal with that part

what helps you feel momentum when your week gets split across too many things?
