hot take

leetcode's ONLY job right now
is helping you pass a big tech interview

for the next 2 years at least

i'm saying this as the founding engineer at my startup

i run the technical interviews
and the behavioral interviews too

i've made some good hires
some okay hires
and some misses

so i've had to think pretty hard about what actually predicts whether someone can build

i'm not saying leetcode is useless

i'm saying people confuse interview prep
with engineering skill

those are NOT the same thing

if i want to know whether someone can actually build
here's what i care about more

1. project depth

i ask what they built
why they chose that architecture
what broke
what tradeoffs they made
what they would change now

2. take-home with ambiguity

i'd rather give someone a take-home where AI CAN help
but one prompt can't finish the job

that tells me way more

i get to see judgment
iteration
debugging
and whether they can think when the path stops being obvious

3. proof of ownership

i'm trying to figure out
did you actually build this
or were you just nearby while it got built

that's the real question

traditional leetcode interviews are broken because they over-index on recall under pressure

real startup engineering is messier than that

it's context
tradeoffs
debugging
communication
and shipping when the spec is still blurry

leetcode is mostly for interview games

building is a DIFFERENT game

if you're hiring engineers in 2026
what do you test for now?
