# Edward's LinkedIn Post System

## Workflow

1. Paste your rough idea into the chat
2. I'll shape it into a post that hits your ICP
3. You review, give feedback, we refine
4. Final post goes into `/posts` with the date

## Folder Structure

```
/posts     → finalized posts, ready to publish (named YYYY-MM-DD-topic.md)
/drafts    → work in progress
profile.md → your context, ammo, goals (keep this updated)
my-achievements.md → your resume
```

## The Goal

Get noticed by YC founders and SF startup recruiters.
Every post should make someone think: "I want this guy on my team."

## How to Give Me a Rough Idea

Just talk. Drop a thought, a story, something that happened, something you built, something you learned.
The rougher the better — that's my job to shape it.

Examples of what works:
- "I just shipped X in Y hours"
- "I learned something weird about LLMs this week"
- "I want to talk about why junior devs struggle with Z"
- "Something frustrated me about how people think about AI"
